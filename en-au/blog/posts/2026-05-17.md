So Sectorfall, the game project we've been working on for 2 years now. Firstly, _what actually is Sectorfall in its current state?_ Currently it's a grand strategy game heavily inspired by games like Empire At War, Hearts of Iron 4, and Stellaris, but it wasn't always like it currently is so let me give you a quick rundown of the history; In November of 2024 the project began as a Jedi Fallen Order style game titled "Shadow of Orion", however we eventually ran into issues with this as we realised the scope was far too large for our team at the time & that Unity simply didn't have the capabilities we needed for a game like this and thus we decided to shutdown the idea before we really got to complete much.

In May of 2025 we then decided to bring back the game; this time in a smaller scope of a Helldivers 2 style game called "Forces of Supremacy". The game would first be developed in Unity 6 before moving to Unreal Engine 5; where we would eventually once again shelve the project. During the game's 2 shelvings, I decided to experiment with a bunch of different game engines to see what could best fit our goals, the ones I tried were:

1. Unreal Engine 5
2. Unreal Engine 4
3. Unreal Engine 3
4. Stride
5. Cry Engine
6. Overload Engine
7. Unity
8. Defold
9. Godot

And eventually I ended on Godot; which we began developing the current form of the game "Sectorfall" in January of 2026 as a multiplayer grand strategy game. So now the question is _when will Sectorfall be available?_ We plan for it to release in an **Early Access** state for _$6.99 AUD_ on Steam & Itch.io in **September of 2026**; although this date is flexable and could be anywhere from August to November 2026; with a **full release** planned for **January 2027**. Continuing on about the development of Sectorfall, throughout January we made major progress; managing to complete the majority of the under the hood systems for the game, following this our development slowed throughout February & March although, we did complete the galaxy map & work on a substantial amount of lore for the game's universe.

In April 2026, we started working on the game's user interface which came together quite rapidly even as we continued to add more features to the game. And that brings us to May 2026; right now we are actively working on completing the features we can implement into the UI and we have also been begining our work on multiplayer which is a large undertaking as we have chosen not to use Steam's multiplayer and instead are creating our own server binary that players will be able to download and host for **free**. The game is primarily driven on JSON files as a means to make the game more modifiable for players (and because it makes our lives easier when making the game).

We will be releasing more information about Sectorfall and its development as we creep closer to its Early Access in September (hopefully) so be aware of that, and make sure to share Sectorfall with your friends and family, a game like this can only succeed if there's a player base. If you're intrested in supporting Sectorfall (and us) we might have a patreon or ByMeACoffee available soon (very big maybe) and if we do we'll also be announcing that here as this will now be our primary place of communication rather than the Discord server.
