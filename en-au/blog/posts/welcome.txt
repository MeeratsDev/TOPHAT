We would like to officially introduce ourselves and share the journey that led to the formation of **Underworld Collective**.

Our roots trace back to 2022, beginning with an independent YouTube series titled _Warfront_, created by our founder, Bodhi Reimer. Inspired by a project from his close colleague Riley Thorpe, _Warfront_ served as our initial venture into narrative world-building.

As our creative ambitions grew, so did our team. Over the past few years, we have expanded our focus beyond video production into game development and digital infrastructure. Collaborating with talented developers & story tellers Justin Miorandi, William Sammon, and Finn Schmidt, we released the roguelike title _Bernard the Bunny_ under the Blankslate Games moniker. We also dedicated significant time to prototyping ambitious sci-fi gaming concepts, navigating the complexities of shifting development from Unity to Unreal Engine 5.

In addition to software development, we recognized a need for independent distribution. This led to the creation and eventual overhaul of our custom streaming platform, which officially launched on New Year's Day 2026 as **WatchFlicker**.

Through these diverse projects; and the inevitable trial and error that accompanies innovation; our core mission became clear. We are not defined by a single medium; rather, we are a unified group of creators dedicated to meaningful storytelling.

In today's media landscape, many of the impactful, character-driven narratives that shaped our industry have been overshadowed by large-scale corporate interests. Underworld Collective was established to challenge that trend, providing an independent space where original, engaging stories can thrive.

To realize this vision, we have officially brought all of our ongoing operations; including our streaming infrastructure, our development team, and our grand strategy title, _Sectorfall_; under one unified independent banner.

Underworld Collective represents a committed team of professionals striving to build high-quality content, games, and platforms that resonate with our audience. We appreciate your support as we begin this new chapter, and we look forward to sharing our upcoming projects with you.
